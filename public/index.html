The Best Movies According to Nokie

My Top 5 Movies of All-Time

1. Back to the Future

Time travel via a Delorean with humor and a complex story = perfection.

2. The Fugitive

Mystery, excitement, and one of the best scores ever!

3. Field of Dreams

More than a story about baseball, cornfields, and ghosts, but those are already compelling enough.

4. The Judge

Grab the tissues as you watch the fractured family dynamics of a family grieving.

5. Flight

An alcoholic pilot saves many lives during a plane crash. Buckle up, this one is full of emotions...and great acting.
